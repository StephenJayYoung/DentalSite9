Meet The Doctor

Timothy D. Williams, DDS

Dr. Williams received his Bachelor of Science degree in biology from Abilene Christian University and his Doctorate of Surgery from the University of Texas at Houston. He consistently increases his knowledge and skills with continuing education courses featuring the latest dental techniques and procedures.
In addition to devoting his time and talents in the community as a volunteer for many organizations, Dr. Williams enjoys spending time with his wife, Elizabeth, and his two daughters.

Dr. Williams is a member of the American Dental Association, the Texas Dental Association, the South Plains District Dental Society, the Academy of General Dentistry, the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry, the American Academy of Dental Sleep Medicine, the International Congress of Oral Implantologists, and the Academy of Laser Dentistry.

Services We Offer

Click on any service below to learn more about our offerings.

Family Care

Family Care


From toddlers to the elderly, our skilled dental team is familiar with the unique needs of every patient, no matter their age. Our goal is to help them develop great oral habits that will last them a lifetime.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic Dentistry


Cosmetic dentistry is one of the most cost-effective ways to improve your appearance. A beautiful, bright, healthy smile can increase self confidence and overall life satisfaction.

One-Visit Crowns

One-Visit Crowns


This technology allows our practice to create tooth-colored porcelain restorations for healthy, beautiful smiles in a single appointment.

Cosmetic Bonding

Cosmetic Bonding


Composite resin is a white, tooth colored material that can be shaded to match your teeth. This material can give you a low-cost improvement in your smile.

Mercury-Free Care

Mercury-Free Care


The common silver filling contains mercury. Dr. Williams uses the highest-quality biocompatible, tooth-colored restorations to create healthy, beautiful smiles.

Implant Dentistry

Implant Dentistry


Dental implants are a great option for replacing missing teeth, improving chewing ability, eliminating bridges, and replacing dentures. Call us today for a complimentary implant consultation.

Smile-Whitening

Smile-Whitening


Many everyday things like coffee, tea and wine can significantly dull teeth. Smile-whitening enables us to significantly brighten your smile for that dazzling white smile you deserve.

TMJ Treatment

TMJ Treatment


If you suffer from headaches or pain, clicking or popping while chewing, there may be problems with your temporomandibular joint (or TMJ). Call us today to schedule a free TMJ treatment consultation.

Sleep Apnea Treatment

Sleep Apnea Treatment


We offer effective, nonsurgical relief from sleep apnea and other sleep-related disorders—including snoring. Ask us how you can get the restful, healthy sleep you’ve been missing.

Full and Partial Dentures

Full and Partial Dentures


Full dentures are an excellent option for people who have lost all of their teeth. They are precisely designed and molded to fit one's mouth. Partial dentures attach either with clasps or to other teeth. Both full or partial dentures allow more comfortable and effective chewing, improve speech, and make you look years younger.

Orthodontics

Orthodontics


A beautifully-aligned natural smile can be obtained both with traditional metal braces and other more subtle options like Invisalign® "invisible" orthodontics. Please contact us today for a free orthodontic consultation!
